# Bollinger Bands Squeeze

Quick visual reference. Terms in the [Glossary → Volatility](./glossary.md).

![Bollinger Bands squeeze: bands contract during low volatility, then expand as price breaks out and rides the upper band, with the middle band as the entry/exit reference](./assets/bollinger-bands-squeeze.svg)

**When I use it**

- **Squeeze** (bands narrow) = low, coiling volatility → expect a move, but direction is unknown.
- Enter on a **close beyond the middle band** in the breakout direction; book profit on a close back through the middle band.
- Confirm with volume and align with structure — squeezes head-fake one way before running the other.
