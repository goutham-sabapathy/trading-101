# Trend Identification

Quick visual reference. Terms in the [Glossary → Market Structure](./glossary.md).

![Uptrend structure: a rising series of higher highs and higher lows, with a trendline connecting the higher lows](./assets/trend-identification-trendline.svg)

**When I use it**

- Core read: uptrend = rising **HH/HL** holding above an up-sloping trendline (mirror for downtrend).
- Confirm health with: price above a rising MA, and breakouts on **rising volume**. Agreement = higher conviction.
- First **lower low** or loss of the trendline/MA = trend in question; see [CHoCH vs BOS](./market-structure-choch-bos.md).
