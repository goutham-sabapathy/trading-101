# Market Structure: CHoCH vs BOS

> Educational reference for reading market structure — BOS (continuation) vs CHoCH (reversal). Quick visual reference.

![Uptrend market structure: successive BOS breaks confirm the uptrend, then a break below the last higher low marks the CHoCH reversal](./assets/choch-bos-uptrend.svg)

![Downtrend market structure: successive BOS breaks confirm the downtrend, then a break above the last lower high marks the CHoCH reversal](./assets/choch-bos-downtrend.svg)

**When I use it**

- **BOS** (break *with* the trend — new HH up / new LL down) → trend continues, trade with it.
- **CHoCH** (break *against* the trend — below the last HL / above the last LH) → first reversal warning, stand aside or flip bias.
- Only weight a CHoCH that lands at a higher-timeframe level; mid-range it's noise.
