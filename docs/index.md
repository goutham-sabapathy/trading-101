# Trading-101

A focused trading reference: capture the **basics and acronyms** once, then go
straight to **specific strategies and best practices**. It is a decision aid, not
a tutorial library — every doc should answer *"what do I do?"* or *"what does this
term mean?"*.

## Start here

- **[Glossary](./common/glossary.md)** — basics and acronyms, defined once. Read this first.
- **[Common Knowledge Base](./common/README.md)** — the categorized index of every doc.
- **[Interactive Tools](./artifacts/)** — the strategy builder and other self-contained apps.

## What's in scope

1. **Basics & acronyms** — the glossary foundation (options, Greeks, volatility, market structure, flow).
2. **Specific strategies** — concrete, screen-and-select playbooks and the rule-based SPY decision system.
3. **Best practices** — risk, position sizing, discipline, and journaling.
4. **Quick visual references** — diagram + a few bullets, linked from the glossary.

**Out of scope:** general education that duplicates what's freely available; proprietary or private execution plans.
