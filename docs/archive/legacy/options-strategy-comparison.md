# Options Strategy Comparison (Earnings Focused)

---

# Iron Condor

Best for:
- Overpriced expected move
- Large IV crush

Max Profit:
Premium received

Max Loss:
Spread width - premium

Wins when:
Stock stays within range

---

# Iron Butterfly

Best for:
- Expect very small move
- Very high IV

Higher reward than condor
Smaller safe zone

---

# Long Straddle

Best for:
- Underpriced expected move
- Expect big surprise

Max Loss:
Premium paid

Profit:
Unlimited upside
Large downside gain

---

# Long Strangle

Cheaper than straddle
Needs larger move to win

---

# Post Earnings Breakout

No IV crush exposure
Trade after direction confirmed
More conservative
